"Let's rewrite it" is the most expensive sentence in software. Most of the time, it's also the wrong one.

## Cut seams, don't burn the house down

A legacy system that runs the business is an asset, not just a liability. The work is usually to find the right seams — the boundaries where you can safely introduce new tooling, automation, and data flows — without a risky big-bang rewrite.

## Tooling around the edges

Process automation, a clean data platform, and internal tools that fit how the team actually works will often deliver more value, faster, than a rebuild that takes two years and lands late. Modernisation is a sequence of defensible steps, not a single act of theatre.
