The purple-faced langur is endemic to Sri Lanka and found nowhere else. It moves through every layer of the forest canopy — at ease deep in cover or out on an exposed branch.

## At home in the whole ecosystem

That's the posture we borrow. Good engineering teams aren't specialists trapped on one layer; they move fluidly between a client's legacy systems and a greenfield AI product, comfortable in both, watchful of the terrain.

## Watchful of the terrain

The langur is agile, but it's also careful. It reads the branches before it commits. That combination — agility and watchfulness — is exactly what building software that has to last actually requires.
